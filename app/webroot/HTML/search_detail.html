Situated in the city center, this luxury hotel is close to Connaught Place, Red Fort, and Jantar Mantar. Also nearby are India Gate and Jama Masjid.

In addition to 4 restaurants, The LaLiT New Delhi features an outdoor pool. Other amenities include a full-service spa and a poolside bar.
